# 🏝️ The Platoon Lagoon

Pick the platoon players who'll crack the MLB lineup.

## What is it?

In baseball, teams often **platoon** players — starting different hitters based on whether the opposing pitcher throws left-handed or right-handed. This app lets you predict which platoon players will make the starting lineup for any given game.

## How to play

1. Browse today's MLB games
2. Pick a game and check the pitching matchup
3. Players tagged with 🏝️ are **platoon candidates** — they have the batting advantage against the opposing pitcher's handedness
4. Select who you think will start
5. Lock in your picks and check back after lineups drop!

## Features

- 📅 Browse games by date (spring training + regular season)
- ⚾ See probable pitchers and their throwing hand
- 🏝️ Auto-tagged platoon candidates based on batter/pitcher handedness
- 📊 Score tracking — see how your picks hold up
- 💾 Picks saved locally

## Tech

- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS — no framework needed
- [MLB Stats API](https://statsapi.mlb.com) — free, no auth
- Deployed on Vercel
